Welcome to nikula on the net!

My name is Nicholas and this is my little site on the world wide web. Nice to meet you!

Oh, also, this website is mobile first and uses semantic tags. Let's make the web accessible to anybody and everybody!

Why did I make this place?

This website is just a fun little self-indulgent project of mine. I thought making a website would be a better alternative to social media, and also I just really like coding :P.

nikula on the net! is the html-ification of what I consider to be 'me', so it is (and always will be) a work in progress. Expect broken pages and empty links.
