About Me

I'm Nicholas (aka soweli Nikula in Toki Pona), and this website is basically my online diary. Thank you for visiting nikula on the net!.

To be honest, I am not really good at describing myself.. To start with the basics, I'm a nerdy highschooler who spends most of their days hunched over a computer.

Most people IRL would recognize me by the bunnyguy I draw all over the classroom whiteboards, or my companion cube plush I carry everywhere with me.

Gender and sexuality is still an enigma to me, but I'm pretty sure I'm nonbinary and somewhere on the aroace spectrum. I have autism and ADHD.

I'm a huge nerd - I love anything computer science-related, especially electronics and Linux.
When I was a kid, I actually wanted to become a professional artist or animator, but I threw that out the window once I discovered Linux.

I discovered Neocities in my freshman year of highschool. During animation class, my friend showed me this cool link to a website named Neocities. I signed up for an account at that moment and haven't looked back since then.
