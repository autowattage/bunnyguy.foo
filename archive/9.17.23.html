I'm Nick (Nicholas) and welcome to my neocities page! Feel free to explore my website :D

This website is always in development, so expect broken websites and messy layouts. You can check out this website's github (version history) and replit (my text editor)

Why did I make snipchu.neocities.org?

Other than my passion for the indie web, old technology, and coding, I wanted to express myself without worrying about large companies and social media.

This website is also a time capsule/archive for me to look back on later.
